A few Words About
Jay Peace

Inspired by the Three Pillars of Creativity, Legacy, and Love, Jay Peace is a Hip-Hop vocalist from Montreal, on a mission to spread his Dynamic, Energetic, and Empowering sound with the world .


Fantasizing about rocking crowds at only 15 years old, Jay Peace realized his artistic drive at an early age. With the dream of inspiring and bringing people together through his own creations, Jay Peace was born.
